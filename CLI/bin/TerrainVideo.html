操作指南：

通过键盘按键可以调整视频参数：
Q X增加factor
W X减少factor

E Y增加factor
R Y减少factor

T Z增加factor
Y Z减少factor

U Heading增加factor
I Heading减少factor

O Tilt增加factor
P Tilt减少factor

D Roll增加factor
F Roll减少factor

G AspectRatio增加factor
H AspectRatio减少factor

J FieldOfView增加factor
K FieldOfView减少factor

Z VideoOpacity增加factor
X VideoOpacity减少factor

D1 factor = 10
D2 factor = 1
D3 factor = 0.1
D4 factor = 0.01

S 保存到Samples\CSharp\bin\adjust.txt
C 显示当前视频
V 隐藏当前视频
